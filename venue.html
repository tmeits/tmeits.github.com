International Summer School

Plant Ecology and Digital Wood Anatomy at Shushensky Bor National Park

1-8 August 2016, Cheremushki, Khakasia, Russian Federation

Venue

The closest airport is the International Airport of Abakan. Organizers from KhTI will meet participants at the airport and after the flight excursion planned around Abakan including lunch. Then participants will be transported to the Cheremushki (116 km from Abakan) by special bus.

Weather in Cheremushki in August.

The average temperature in August is 16.4°C. The mean daily maximum 22.4°C and mean daily minimum 11.4°C. In August in Cheremushki receives of 82.2 mm rainfall. The weather in the mountains is unpredictable and heavy rains are possible. There are a lot of slippery rocks so you can bring trekking boots.

August is the ending of the mosquito and tick season, it is better to be prepared and to take some repellent with you.
