TRACE 2017 will be held on 16–21 May 2017 in the conference center “Volna” in Svetlogorsk, Kaliningrad region, Russian Federation.



The organizers are Siberian Federal University (Krasnoyarsk, Russia), Baltic Federal University (Kaliningrad, Russia), and Association for Tree-Ring Research.



TRACE 2017 develops the TRACE traditions and seeks to strengthen the networking and scientific exchange of scientists and students involved in the study of tree-rings. Its aim is to present and discuss new discoveries and approaches in tree-ring science.

The scope of the meeting includes all fields of dendrochronology and its application in archaeology, climatology, geomorphology, glaciology, fire history, forest dynamics, ecology, plant and wood anatomy, hydrology and physiology, including the use of stable isotopes. The scientific program includes oral and poster sessions, short courses.
