Travel to Svetlogorsk


For those considering taking a flight for the conference, the closest airport is the Kaliningrad international airport Khrabrovo. Flight to Kaliningrad currently ranges from a minimum of 150 euros (Berlin) to 400 euros (Spain, Italy).

There is no direct or efficient public transportation between Kaliningrad airport and Svetlogorsk. We therefore plan to organize buses between the airport and the venue on May, 16-17, 2017, and in the other direction on May, 21, 2017. A taxi takes about one hour and will cost about 30 euros.

To efficiently coordinate the bus schedule we need information about your arrivals time. Please, as soon as you book the flight let us know about the flight and time of your arrival to the airport. Deadline for submitting your flight schedule (to TRACERussia2017@mail.ru.) is April 30 2017. A bus schedule will be posted online by May 5th

Russian visa


To get Russian visa, you need to apply the Russian Embassy in your country with your valid international passport and Invitation letter. Please, check the rules in the Russian embassy in your country to learn what else you need to submit and how long it takes to get Russian visa. Baltic Federal University will provide you the required Invitation letter.

When you plan your staying in Kaliningrad region please remember
that your staying in Kaliningrad region must not ne longer than 7 (seven) days


To get the visa invitation letter you are to fill in the special form, then send it together with a clear pdf copy of your international passport (all pages) and your surface post address to TRACE2017visa@mail.ru not later than March, 10, 2017. You will get the invitation letter from Baltic Federal University: first a pdf copy to your e-mail address and then a hard original copy to the surface post address.
