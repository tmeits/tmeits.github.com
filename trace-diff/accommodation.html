ACCOMMODATION

Accommodation for conference participants at a favorable price is available in the hotel “Volna” in Svetlogorsk. The hotel “Volna” can offer both single and double rooms at a very convenient price

Please, visit the webpage of the hotel “Volna”
http://www.hotelvolna.ru/nomera


  1. single room – about 35 euros per night
  2. double room – about 49 euros per night
  3. more comfortable double rooms – about 65–70 euros per night

To reserve a room you need to send a message to e-mail: TRACE2017hotel@mail.ru. In the message please communicate - the date for you staying in the hotel, and with whom you share the room when you book a double room.



The reservation starts February, 1st.
The rooms are dispatched by “first come first served” method.
