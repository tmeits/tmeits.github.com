VENUE

The conference will be held in Svetlogorsk, Kaliningrad, at the conference hall in the hotel "Volna".

The accomodation is also available in the hotel "Volna". The hotel has restaraunts, bars, sauna, gym.

Svetlogorsk is a resort town at Baltic sea shore. The hotel "Volna" is at five minute walking distance from the sea.

The weather in May is pretty comfortable. Daytime air temperture is about 17 Celcius, nighttime temperature is about 9 Celcius.

Water is too cold to swim (10 Celcius).

The town is surrounded steppe-forest vegetation.
