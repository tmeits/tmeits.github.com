TRAVEL AND VISA INFO

Visa Info

To get Russian visa, you need to apply the Russian Embassy in your country with your valid international passport and invitation letter. Please, check the rules in the Russian embassy in your country to learn what else you need to submitt and how long it takes to get Russian visa. Baltic Federal University will provide the invitation letter that you need to submitt when you apply for Russin visa at the Russian embassy in your country.

To get the visa invitation letter you are to fill in the special form and send a clear pdf copy of your international passport (all pages) together with the information on your surface post address to TRACE2017visa@mail.ru not later than March, 10, 2017. You will get the invitation letter from Baltic Federal University: first a pdf copy at your e-mail address and then a hard original copy to the surface post address in ten days.

To reach Kaliningrad: take a flight to the Kaliningrad international airport


  1. http://www.farecompare.com/flights/Paris-PAR/Kaliningrad-KGD/market.html#quote
  2. http://www.edreams.com/flights/rome-kaliningrad/ROM/KGD/

The price varies from 150 euros (Berlin) to 400 euros (Spain, Italy). There no shutle bus from Kaliningrad airport to Svetlogorsk.
So it is possible to get taxi. It takes about one hour and costs about thirty euros.

We plan to provide buses that will meet the participants at the airport in May, 16-17, 2017.
And also we will provide buses for participans to get the airport in May, 21, 2017.

To develop the schedule for these buses we need information on your arrivals the sooner the better. Please, as soon as you book the flight let us know about the flight and time of your arrival to the airport.
